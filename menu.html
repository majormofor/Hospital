Website Guide

How to Use This Website

Getting Started

When you first arrive at our website, you will see a homepage with links to various sections of the site. Use the navigation nav bar to view various pages such as staffs, about us, contact us (which you can use to send direct email to Mofor practice care email ), read Patients Testimonial, login (which brings a dropdown-menu to signin for either as a patient or as a Staff). You Could also read Our Frequently Asked Questions at the bottom of the page.



Creating an Account

As a new Patient, you are requested to Register with the link New patient, upon successful registration, you are redirected to the Patient Login Page



Patient Login Page

On the Patient Login Page, you are instructed to input your Email and Password, you used to register as this will alway be used to login to access special care in Mofor Practice Care



Patient Homepage

After successful Logging In, You are directed to your homepage where Patients can do the following.

  1. Home- Create Testimonial by a write a testmonial link, also read a testmonial and Blog Post.
  2. Profile - View your profile Details.
  3. View Prescription - that is if a GP/doctor has prescribe one for you.
  4. Book an Appointment
  5. View Appointment- if they has being accepted, it will be green, else Red for rejected.
  6. View Medical Test Record- such Urinalysis, Stool Test, Imaging testing, Blood Count, Lipid Profile Test

Note that whichever form you fill, upon successful submission, it takes you back to the Patient Homepage (Home)



Log Out

Upon login out, it takes you to the first page you entered when visiting the site

If you have any questions or concerns about our site, you can contact us by clicking the "Contact Us" link in the navbar, email us and one of our team members will get back to you as soon as possible.
